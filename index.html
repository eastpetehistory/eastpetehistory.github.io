Welcome to East Petersburg Historical Society

About Us

Founded in 1974, our mission is uncovering, preserving and sharing the history of our community and the surrounding area.

Our Museum at 1905 State Street is the historic Wolf House located on the NE corner Lemon and State streets in East Petersburg with free parking behind the building off of Lemon St.

We are open to the public each first Saturday of the month from 9:00 AM to noon. Visits are also welcome by request or any time you see our OPEN flag out, just walk right in the front door.
