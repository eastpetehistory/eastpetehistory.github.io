About Us

Founded in 1974, our non-profit mission is uncovering, preserving and sharing the history of our community and the surrounding area.

Our Museum at 1905 State Street is the historic Wolf House located on the NE corner of Lemon and State streets in East Petersburg with free parking behind the building off Lemon Street.
