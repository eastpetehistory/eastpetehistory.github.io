EPHS's Museum Index Headers Explained
Field Value
EPHS#East Petersburg Historical Society Record (Index) Number
If the number is a link, hover over it, then position your curser over the image of the folder that appears just below and left-click.
This will show you a Google Drive folder view of files in the record. You can change the list appearace from file name to image in the upper left; double click a file or image to open it.
LinkIndividual file within the record
If the number is a link, hover over it to see the content, then position your curser over the content that appears just below to open the file
TypeIdentifies the general category or format of the item, designating material or intellectual form, reflecting its primary documentary or physical format
QtyQuantity, sometimes but seldomly duplicates
DonorPerson(s) or organization who gifted the item
Subjectcategorizes the item by its main historical topic, place, or activity, such as education, transportation, local history, or social life
Type = what kind of object it is; Subject = what itâ€™s about
Descriptionbriefly explains what the item is, what it depicts or contains, and any distinguishing features or context not captured elsewhere -
answers one or more of: What is it? What does it show or contain? Why is it notable?
CircaAssociated year (if a range of years, the earliest)
LocationRoom designation within the Wolf House (WH) Museum 1 = 1st Level, 2 = 2nd Level (upstairs), 4 = 3rd Level (attic), B = Basement, G = Garage, F = Front (gift shop), D = Dining (middle) room, K = Kitchen, O = Office (upstairs front room), C = Closet, L = Library (upstairs middle room), A = Archive (upstairs back room), WC = Water Closet
UnitIdentifies the specific storage furniture or display piece where the item is physically or digitally housed
LevelShelf # or Drawer # or compass direction
Container LabelContent indicator; Note that a container may be shared, but not have more than one label
SizeGeneral dimensions of the container
VerifiedChecked if the item has been seen in its place in recent times
